Explains scheduled Pix transfers, which let users program transfers automatically for future dates while funds remain in the account until the exact scheduled day. Covers how scheduled Pix differs from instant transfers, typical processing times between 6-9 AM on business days, and steps for creating, managing, and canceling scheduled transfers through Stone's account platform.
